촬영된 이미지는 Gemini AI에 의해 처리되며, 이메일 데이터는 Firebase를 통해 백업됩니다. 다른 제3자 서비스에는 전달되지 않습니다.

1. Information We Collect

BakeLog requests access to your device camera and your email. No other personal information is collected. Email is used to back up recipe information you provide.

2. Purpose of Camera Usage

The camera permission is used solely to capture photos of recipe books. These images are sent to our AI service (Gemini) for automatic recipe extraction.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any photos on our servers or devices after processing. All images are processed temporarily and discarded immediately. Email is securely stored using Firebase.

4. Third-Party Services

The captured images are processed by Gemini AI, and email data is backed up via Firebase. No other third-party service receives your data.

5. User Rights

You can revoke camera access at any time in your device settings. BakeLog will not retain any images after processing. Email deletion can be requested anytime.
