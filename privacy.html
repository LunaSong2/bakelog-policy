Privacy Policy for BakeLog

Effective date: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

BakeLog requests access to your device camera. We do not collect any other personal information.

2. Purpose of Camera Usage

The camera permission is used solely to capture photos of recipe books. These images are sent to our AI service (Gemini) for automatic recipe extraction.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any photos on our servers or devices after processing. All images are processed temporarily and then discarded immediately.

4. Third-Party Services

The captured images are processed by Gemini AI for recipe extraction. No other third-party service receives your photos.

5. User Rights

You can revoke camera access at any time in your device settings. BakeLog will not retain any images after processing.
